About our Services

Picknfreight can provide you with multiple transportation options anywhere in Greater Manchester. We will be able to accommodate your needs with custom tailored solution. Picknfreight maintains fleet of luton trucks and bikes of various sizes and configurations to meet your daily pick up, delivery and moving needs. We specialise in the small and large item shipping service. We'll pick it up and deliver it to your new homes or offices or store with our intermodal bikes or trucks. And if you need large freezers or shelf units delivered for remodel or an oversize item for promotional display we can carry and offload those too. By reading the information below you can see how our transportation services stack up against your current service Trucking. We guarantee to meet or beat the price of any other carrier. Just show us your current contract and we'll show you how we can do better. We are Greater manchester regional carrier so our drivers know the area and are not as fatigued as long haul carriers. We have variety of trailer sizes and types so odds are good that we can always provide you with dedicated runs from pickup to delivery at your homes/offices/facility instead of less than truckload runs that must make stops for multiple customers Our bikes and trucks are equipped with state of the art Satellite Communications Systems which allow our drivers access to online maps two way communications with dispatch and email messages. You and the driver can easily communicate. Our trucks are equipped with Pre Pass electronic systems so our drivers can bypass most delays along the route. We know the best routes in and out of your neighborhoods and the best ways to maneuver up to your delivery door. And we have trucks and bikes of all sizes to accommodate your needs whether large and small. We take the stress of delivery and moving away from you. So whether you need to drop off a small package, or you are moving homes and offices, be sure to have the best hands to cater for you needs.
